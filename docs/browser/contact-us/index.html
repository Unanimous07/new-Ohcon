Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with OHCON?

You can apply to volunteer through our "Get Involved" page. We have various opportunities available for different skill sets and time commitments.

What is One Health?

One Health is an integrated approach that recognizes the interconnection between human, animal, and environmental health. It promotes collaboration across disciplines to achieve optimal health outcomes.

How can I support OHCON's mission?

You can support us by volunteering, making a donation, spreading awareness about One Health principles, or partnering with us on initiatives.

Do you offer educational programs?

Yes, we offer various educational programs focused on One Health principles, community health, and professional development for healthcare workers.
