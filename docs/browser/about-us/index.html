About OHCON

We connect human, animal, and environmental health through community-led programs, evidence-based research, and sustainable partnerships across Malawi.

Our Mission

To protect and improve the health and livelihoods of Malawians through integrated, community-led One Health solutions that connect human, animal, and environmental well-being.

Our Vision

A resilient Malawi where people, animals, and ecosystems thrive togetherfree from preventable diseases and environmental risks, supported by locally owned, multi-sectoral systems.

Why One Health Matters

Malawi's economy and food security are tightly linked to livestock and natural resources. When animal disease, poor sanitation, or environmental degradation occur, they can quickly threaten human health and livelihoods.

One Health is the collaborative approach that prevents and responds to these cross-sectoral threatsimproving outcomes more effectively than isolated programs.

Our Story

A journey of passion, collaboration, and unwavering commitment to One Health in Malawi

The Beginning (2022)

Founded in 2022, OHCON emerged from a shared vision to address complex health challenges facing Malawi through an integrated One Health approach. Our founders recognized that the health of humans, animals, and the environment are deeply interconnected.

Our Growth

Starting with a small team of passionate healthcare professionals, we've grown into a dynamic organization that collaborates with communities, educational institutions, and healthcare providers across Malawi.

Looking Forward

Today, we continue to expand our reach and impact, working towards our vision of a healthier, more resilient Malawi where One Health principles guide all health-related decisions and actions.
