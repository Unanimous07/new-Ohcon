Our Programs Get Involved

Our Mission

Promoting One Health principles to improve the well-being of communities through interdisciplinary collaboration and holistic approaches.

Our Vision

Building a healthy and resilient Malawian society where human, animal, and environmental health are interconnected and prioritized for the benefit of all.

Our Goals

1

Disease Prevention and Control

To reduce the incidence and transmission of infectious diseases, including zoonotic diseases, through targeted prevention and control strategies.

2

Antimicrobial Resistance Mitigation

To combat antimicrobial resistance (AMR) by promoting responsible antimicrobial use and stewardship practices in human and animal healthcare settings.

3

Environmental Conservation

To protect and preserve natural ecosystems while promoting sustainable practices that benefit both human and animal health.

4

Community Engagement

To empower communities through education and participation in One Health initiatives, fostering sustainable health practices.
