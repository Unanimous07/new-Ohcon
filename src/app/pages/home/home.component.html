Bridging Health, Shaping Futures

OHCON is a Malawian NGO implementing practical One Health solutions — linking human, animal and environmental health to prevent disease, protect livelihoods and strengthen local systems. We deliver school programs, integrated clinics, WASH interventions, AMR stewardship and policy engagement—working with government, universities and communities.

We bring together healthcare professionals, veterinarians, environmental scientists, and community leaders to create sustainable solutions for Malawi's most pressing health challenges.
